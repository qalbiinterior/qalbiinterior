About Us

QALBI INTERIOR DESIGN is a small-scale fabrication unit started in the year 2021. With 5 years of dedicated industry experience, and under the leadership of our Proprietor, Mrs. Meena Jangra, we have quickly taken a strong hold in the fabrication work of spare part container offices, aluminium structure glazing, and other industrial fabrication and construction projects within a short span of time.

We provide expert interior & exterior services including ACP cladding, glass facades, aluminium work, and modular interiors. We lay a strong emphasis on manufacturing our products at par with the latest market developments so as to achieve optimum client satisfaction.

Operating nationwide, we take pride in manufacturing superior quality products at affordable prices. Our ultimate commitment is to consistently deliver exceptional quality work, modern designs, and on-time project completion.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you provide services in?

We operate nationwide across India. Our manufacturing unit is located in Ashok Vihar Phase 3, Gurgaon, and we are capable of executing both residential and commercial projects effectively across the country.

Do you manufacture your own materials?

Yes, Qalbi Interior Design is primarily a fabrication unit. We manufacture superior quality products (like modular kitchens, wardrobes, and structural glazing components) at affordable prices to maintain strict quality control.

How long does a typical interior project take?

The timeline varies significantly depending on the scope of work. A standard modular kitchen might take 2-3 weeks, whereas complete residential interiors or large exterior structural glazing can take 6-12 weeks. We provide exact timelines during the design phase.

Do you offer both Interior and Exterior services?

Absolutely. We specialize in exterior fabrication (ACP cladding, HPL, glass facades, aluminium structures) as well as premium interior solutions (modular kitchens, false ceilings, office interiors).
